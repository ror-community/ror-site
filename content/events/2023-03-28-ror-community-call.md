### ROR Community Call - March 2023
- Date and time: Tuesday, March 28th, 2023 at [3pm UTC](https://www.timeanddate.com/worldclock/fixedtime.html?msg=ROR+Community+Call+March+2023&iso=20230328T15&p1=1440&ah=1) (1 hour)
